Pasta Primavera

A mixture of shrimp, pasta, and fresh vegetables make up this slightly spicy dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pachage rigatoni pasta
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 can can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 pounds shrimp, peeled and deveined

Stems to Make The Dish

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook rigatoni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 13 minutes. Drain.
  2. While the pasta is boiling cook the veggies so they will finish at the same time as the pasta. To do so, heat olive oil in a 12-inch (and deep) skillet over medium-high heat. Add red onion and carrot and saut√© 2 minutes.
  3. Pour veggies into now empty pasta pot or a serving bowl, add drained pasta, drizzle in lemon juice, season with a little more salt as needed and toss while adding in pasta water to loosen as desired. Toss in 1/4 cup parmigiano and parsley then serve with remaining parmesan on top.
